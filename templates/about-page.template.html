About

Band Browser is a project to organize the youtube music videos of various artists into a single, accessible location, with additional features to make searching/playing those videos more convenient. The project dances close to the edge of copyright infringement, and is liable to disappear at any second. We highly encourage you to purchase the music that this site links from a venue that will benefit the artists such as iTunes or Amazon. Thank you for visiting us.
